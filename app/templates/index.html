About Us




Our website is one stop solution finder for the farmers to enhance their yield by getting suggestion for the type of crop/ fertilizer to be used for their land with atmost accuracy and also to detect any leaf disease and give the suggestions to protect their crop.

A trip to a friend's home town gave us an insight into the agriculture and farming techniques. After a short interaction with the local farmers, we understood that the old farming techniques such as organic fertilizers made with cow dung, neem oil to prevent pesticides does not work efficiently as in old times because of various reasons like, irregular rainfall, global warming, lack of nutrients in the soil, resistance towards fertilizers.

Also, most importantly the nutrient value in soil has been drastically changed in the past decade leading to the need of advanced cultivation methods to attain a higher yield in order to balance the demand for food of our rising population. At that moment, we realised as engineers we could intervein and blend technology with farming to enhance the productivity and this website is a outcome of the thought.
